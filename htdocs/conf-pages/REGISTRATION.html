Registration

Two options for attending the conference and/or press conference:

IN-PERSON:

In-person attendance is limited to 120 farmers. The registration fee of $180 includes meals (see program), conference materials, and transportation to/from field day. Lodging is not included in the registration fee and hotel arrangements (LODGING) are the responsibility of the attendee. Registration (Opens April 7) and will close June 30.

ONLINE:

This will be a great option for non-farmers and farmers not able to attend in-person. All components of the conference will be live-streamed, except for the field day portion on Wednesday morning. You must register (Registration opens April 7) for attending via live-stream also. Registered online participants will receive the weblink and conference materials electronically prior to the conference.There is no fee for online attendance.

Important additional information for all:

During the registration process you will be asked to create a password. Registration receipts will be provided by email. If a valid email address is not provided during the registration process you will not receive confirmation of your registration.

Cancellations requesting a refund must be received before midnight, June 30, 2014. Cancellations received after that date will not be refunded. For registration questions (receipts, cancellation of registration, status of registration) contact Registration Services at (515) 294-6222 or registrations@iastate.edu.

Scholarships for Next Generation Farmers

Scholarships are available for future farmers. Scholarships will cover travel, meals, registration, and hotel for one night. Apply online by May 15. For a flier explaining the process of applying for a scholarship, click here: FLIER. To go directly to the online application, click on this link or copy and paste it into your browser: http://bit.ly/1gFHA17.

MEDIA:

A press conference will be held Tuesday, August 5, at 10:30 a.m., in the Meadow Room in the Gateway Conference Center. The press conference will also be live-streamed. Register for the live-stream or in-person press conference and to receive a press packet HERE (Link will be live later in April) Remarks will be made by:

  • Lois Wright Morton, Sustainable Corn Project Director, Iowa State University
  • Wendy Wintersteen, Dean of the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences at Iowa State
  • Ernie Shea, Project Coordinator of 25 x 25
  • Secretary of Agriculture Tom Vilsack (invited)

Press are also encourage to attend conference proceedings via live streaming. A few spaces are available for farm press who want to attend the conference in-person. Please contact Lynn Laws , 515-294-7380 for more information if you wish to attend the conference in-person.

Press planning to attend the Press Conference in-person are not required to register. However, all media registering in advance will receive the press release and conference materials in advance, electronically.
