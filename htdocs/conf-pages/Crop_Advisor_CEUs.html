Crop Advisor CEUs

NEW! Resilient Agriculture Conference attendance is now open to crop advisors! Certified Crop Advisor (CCA) continuing education units are available, with your application to ASA after participating in conference sessions, either on-line or in-person.

Up to 8.5 Certified Crop Adviser (CCA) continuing education credits can be applied for if you attend the conference in-person; 6.5 if you attend online. Credits are across 5 categories with the majority in soil and water management and crop management and is based on the sessions attended:


In-Person Attendance, with advance registration
Tuesday: 1.5 SWM, 1 CM
Wednesday: 2.5 SWM, 1 CM, 0.5 IPM (NOTE: 2 credits for field day activities are still pending approval from ASA)
Thursday: 1 SWM, 0.5 NM, 0.5 PD

Online Attendance, with advance registration
Tuesday: 1.5 SWM, 1 CM
Wednesday: 1.5 SWM, 0.5 IPM
Thursday: 1 SWM, 0.5 NM, 0.5 PD

ASA Certified Crop Advisor CEU application forms will be available at the conference for people attending in-person. For people attending online, CEU application forms will be available online.
