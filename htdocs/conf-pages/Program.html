Program

Our conference is designed exclusively for farmers: talk with other farmers, ag scientists and industry leaders to learn and discuss:

  • Latest science on climate impacts on agriculture
  • Adaptation strategies for corn/soybean-based systems
  • Field day: demonstration plots with time to talk to scientists
  • New and emerging technologies for farm management
