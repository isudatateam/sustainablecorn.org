Presenting Speed Science

These "Speed Science" presentation were made at this project's 2012 annual conference in Wooster, Ohio.

This "Sustainable Corn" (CSCAP) project assembles a team from 10 Land Grant Universities and two USDA Agricultural Research Service laboratories across nine states in the Heartland (Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Michigan, Minnesota, Missouri, Ohio, South Dakota, and Wisconsin). The team is comprised of biophysical and social scientists including soil scientists and agronomists, sociologists, economists, agricultural engineers, modelers, and climatologists as well as educators and extension field specialists. At the Team's 2012 conference, these scientists explained the work they are doing for this project to all others on the team through these brief "Speed Science" presentations and fact sheets. The videos of the presentations and the fact sheets are approved for use in education, research and extension..
